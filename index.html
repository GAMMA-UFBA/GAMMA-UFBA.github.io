GAMMA

Statistics. Modelling. Data.
On August 2012, an ambient for research and discussion of multivariate statistical methods was created within the Federal University of Bahia.
The contributor of the research group, GAMMA, are business professionals, students of Institutional Scientific Initiation Scholarship Program, Masters and doctoral students of the Industrial Engineering Program (PEI) and Masters in Environment, Water and Sanitation (MAASA), and teachers and students interested in the subject.
The GAMMA research room is located on the 6th floor of the UFBA Polytechnic School in room 6.1.17. Equipped with 10 computers and a coffee machine (indispensable), we deal daily with challenges involving time series, machine learning, multivariate statistics and so on.
